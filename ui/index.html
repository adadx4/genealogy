    How to use it

    • Choose one event at a time and enter as much location and date detail as you know.
    • Every collection that overlaps your filters is listed — most specific first.
    • Empty fields act as wildcards. Clear the county/parish to broaden the search.
    • Each result links to the collection's search page (or its homepage where no deep link exists).
